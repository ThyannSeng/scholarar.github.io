Who is

Scholarar?

Scholarar is an innovative technology venture that acts as a bridge for students to achieve their dream scholarship. The Scholarar team is truly committed to supporting young people seeking scholarships for both domestic and international studies. With the services and products the specialists offer, you can make your scholarship dreams a reality.

Our Vision

Scholarar's vision is to maximize successful Cambodian talents for overseas education to bring knowledge to develop our country.

Our Mission

To achieve this vision, Scholarar strives daily to match Cambodian talents with overseas scholarships.
